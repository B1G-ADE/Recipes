Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's a simple yet delicious meal that is quick to prepare and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 8 slices of bacon or pancetta, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of dry white wine
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons of grated Romano cheese (optional)
