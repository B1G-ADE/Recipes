Pepperoni Pizza

Description

This classic pepperoni pizza is a beloved favorite made with a crispy crust, savory tomato sauce, and generous amounts of mozzarella and pepperoni.

Ingredients

Pizza Sauce

  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/2 (12oz) can of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper

Pizza Dough

  • 3 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour (add more if needed)
  • 2 envelopes of active dry yeast
